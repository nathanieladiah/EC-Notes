 * Fire is not emotional passion or force or an outworking of our personality. It's not more productivity or religion based.
* It's not situationally based

Fire is:
1. A fervour and passion birthed within us by God.
	1 Kings 18:25
	 Where does the fire come from. It's life provided by God.
	 The fire does not require frantic human energy.

2. Corporate illumination producing Accurate Migration.
	Exodus 13:22
	 the emphasis is that there is guidance.

3. It is a tool of purification.
	 Malachi 3:2

	 The fire is insisting on a complete and final removal of lingering elements of all Pharisee spirits.

4. The source of the fear of the Lord
	 Hebrews 12:28-29
	 There is no shadow or hypocrisy.
	 Hebrews 10:26

5. A testing of our connection to the Apostolic Grace
	 1 Corinthians 3:10-13
	 We need to apply the teachings in our own personal lives.

6. A protection factor for the people of God at the End of Time
	 Revelation 11:3-6
