# Guarding our Hearts
[[Graham King]]

In the context of the "_Love One Another_"

> "If he can't get to us externally, he tries internally"
>
>"Give no place or ground to the enemy"

- Ephesians 4:25-27

We must guard against becoming resentful of those we love the most.
Because we feel there is an inherent injustice in the relationship.
* How I have been treated... or...
* The demand that is put on me... or...
* A lack of patience with my faults and failings

## The Issue of Trust

A big point of confrontation from the Lord is _do I truly **trust** my brother?_
Remember the **Phalanx** depends on trust in the soldier next to you.
