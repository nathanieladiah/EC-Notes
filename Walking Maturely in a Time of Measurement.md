## Inquiry and Intimacy

Measurement requires a comparison against a known standard or design. This means there needs to be inquiry and intimacy.

This is also an expression of otherness, since the river that flows through us affects others.

> Father, give us a listening heart. Like what you did to Saul on his way to Damascus.

Emphasis first on **intimacy** not works; **interaction** not action; **listening** not demanding.

> We want clarity of listening and no distortion. Clear instruction to us in this season.
