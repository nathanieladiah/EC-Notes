## Elements of our Unseen World

To walk on the Highway to Zion is to walk into greater and greater mystery and further and further away from the rational, natural order of reality. ***Strategic Disconnection***

* Being Released and delivered from the governing power of the primary elements of this world order: Wealth; Status; Popularity; Recognition etc.
* Receiving the knowledge, guidance, wisdom, direction and desires that come from the Upper Realm.
	* _"I don't do anything unless I hear from my Father"_
* Engaged in active preparation for life in Immortality - The thrust to full Maturity.
* At the same time Functioning with perfect efficiency here with success on all fronts (Daniel).

Jesus was functional in his disconnection. A real human being.
