The Book of Revelation...

## Part 1
The book will uncover the mystery of spiritual things unfolding in the earth in the spasm of Finishing.

The End is non-earthly. It cannot be affected by any power on the earth.

The Book triggers a significant attitude shift.
We want to be a partner bringing the purpose of God into fruition.

### Who was the vessel: Who is John?

* Trusted by Jesus: 
	* John 19:26
	* We want God to have the same trust in us. 
* The disciple whom Jesus "loved"
	* John 13:23 / 19:26 / 20:2 / 21:7 / 21:20
	* This should be our desire; to be loved by Christ.
* One of the 3 allowed into deep sacred spiritual transactions
	* Mark 5:37
	* He did not disrupt the flow of the grace.
